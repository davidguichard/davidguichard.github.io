The Chromebooks have one printer preinstalled, with IP address 192.168.234.50. You only need these instructions if somehow the printer has been deleted from the Chromebook, or if you need to use the second printer with IP address 192.168.234.51.

Click on the icon at the bottom right corner of the screen:

Click on the gear icon:

Click "Advanced":

Click "Printing":

Click "Printers":

Click the icon to the right of "Add Printer":

Brother 2370DW printers

Fill in a name for the printer; it's a good idea to include the IP address in the name, either 192.168.234.50 or 192.168.234.51 (on a label on the printer). Fill in the address to match. Then click "Add".

You should see the new printer in the list.
